Black Panther has the greatest mysticism associated with it. It is the symbol of the feminine, the dark mother, the dark of the moon. It is the symbol for the life and power of the night. It is a symbol of the feminine energies manifest upon the earth. It is often a symbol of darkness, death, and rebirth from out of it. To the Tucano Indians of the amazon, the roar of the jaguar was the roar of thunder. Thus, the Black Panther was the God of Darkness and cause eclipse by swallowing the sun.
